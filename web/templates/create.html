💡 Tips for Creating NACHA Files

🏦 Routing Numbers

  • Must be exactly 9 digits
  • Use valid ABA routing numbers
  • Destination: receiving bank
  • Origin: your bank

💰 Amount Format

  • Enter amounts in cents
  • $1.00 = 100 cents
  • $1,234.56 = 123456 cents
  • No decimal points needed
